The Best Movies According to Scott

My top three movies of all-time

Lord of the Rings, The Fellowship of the Ring

The first movie of the greatest trilogy of all-time. A cinematic masterpiece.

Lord of the Rings, The Two Towers

The second movie of the greatest trilogy of all-time. My go to when I don't have time for all three.

Lord of the Rings, The Return of the King

The third and final movie of the greatest trilogy of all-time. Musically, the best of the three.
